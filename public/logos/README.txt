PARTNER LOGOS
=============

Drop the real logo image files here with these exact names and they will
automatically replace the placeholder crests on the landing page:

  university.png         — University / academic partner
  parco-scientifico.png  — Parco Scientifico (science / technology park)
  who.png                — WHO / UN health organization

Recommended: transparent PNG or SVG, ~120px tall, trimmed whitespace.
To add more partners, edit src/components/TrustLogos.jsx (PARTNERS array).
Until a file is present, a styled fallback crest with the partner name is shown.
